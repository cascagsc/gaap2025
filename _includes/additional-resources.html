AMP-UP

  • AMP-UP (Astronomy Mentorship Program for Upcoming Postdocs) is a mentorship program in which current and previous prize postdoctoral fellows provide professional and academic advice and support to PhD students performing astronomy-related research.

  • AMP-UP is currently accepting Mentee applications for the 2025/2026 application cycle. The deadline is June 15th, 2025.
